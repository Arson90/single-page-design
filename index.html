About game...

Max Payne is a third-person shooter video game series developed by Remady Entertainment (Max Payne and Max Payne 2) and Rockstar Studios (Max Payne 3). The website contains a description of only the first part of the game.

DEA agent and former NYPD officer Max Payne attempts to catch people who are responsible for murdering his wife and child, as well as framing him for the murder of his partner, Alex Balder. Murderers are under the influence of an unknown drug called Valkyr. During the game Max meets a few people like a Russian mafia gangster, called Vladimir Lem, and Mona Sax. Max soon discovers that the drug is Valhalla's government project.
